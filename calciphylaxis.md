# calciphylaxis

- related: [[Nephrology]], [[hypocalcemia and electrolytes]]
- tags: #nephrology

---

- Avoid Ca supplement in high phos

## When to consider

- Ca meq/ml X phosphate mmol/ml
- when Ca x P > 80 at risk
- if >200 then calciphylaxis

## Treatment

- Use sodium thiosulfate
	- SE: can cause metabolic acidosis: CO2 between 15-18
- Can also use vitamin K
