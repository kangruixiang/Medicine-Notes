# post op pacing initial settings

- related: [[cardiac pacemaker]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- rate: pace at 90 - 100 to avoid over distending the heart (less refill time)
- can also use back mode of 50-60 if there is native rhythm
- output usually at 10 mA. Use 2x+1 amplitude of what is needed for capture
- A max output: 20
- V max output: 25
- PR interval: adjust in heart block patient. PR usually much longer in native rhythm than set rhythm
- tracking: QRS for every P
- upper limit: commonly at 110-120 to have upper V pacing. Usually set together with tracking to avoid SVT
