# station 11R has superior and inferior nodes

- related: [[Chest lymph nodes and where to find them]]
- tags: #permanent
---

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230708080905.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230708080418.png)

- 11R has superior and inferior nodes
- superior node: right after upper lobe takeoff
- inferior node: right between RML and RLL

## EBUS

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230708080557.png)

- superior 11R is much easier to find: navigate to right below RUL take off and look at the bifurcation between RUL and BI around 10 o'clock.
- navigate to right main and then as deep as you can go in right lower lobe
- do a 180 sweep to look for inferior 11R
