# azithromycin could be helpful in bronchiectasis patients with over 3 exacerbations per year

- related: [[chronic bronchiectasis]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Avoid frequent exacerbations at all cost.

Azithromycin 500 mg 3x/week or 250 mg daily can reduce exacerbations and inflammations (BLESS, BAT, EMBRACE). [^1]

Check non-TB mycobacterium before starting chronic azithromycin to minimize risk of MAC resistance ([[use azithromycin with caution in patients with nonTB mycobacterium infections]]).

- monitor hearing and vestibular toxicity.[^2]
