# eosinophilic pneumonia lung biopsy shows eosinophils, giant cells, bronchiolitis obliterans

- related: [[acute and chronic eosinophilic pneumonia AEP and CEP]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Lung ==biopsy== usually shows interstitial and alveolar eosinophils with multinucleated giant cells and associated bronchiolitis obliterans with organizing pneumonia.[^1]

The size of forceps transbronchial biopsy may be too small, and transbronchial cryotherapy may be difficult.[^2]
