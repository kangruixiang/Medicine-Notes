# tpa contraindications

- related: [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #literature
---

<!-- tpa contraindications b:1454532807751-->

Contraindications to fibrinolysis with tPA for stroke include:

- Presence of active internal bleeding
- Bleeding diathesis (eg, platelets <100,000/µL)
- Presence of intracranial hemorrhage on CT scan
- Intracranial surgery in the last 3 months
- Blood pressure >185/110 mm Hg

Contraindications for PE includes:

- intracranial neoplasms
- recent intracranial or spinal surgery or trauma
- a history of hemorrhagic stroke
- or a nonhemorrhagic stroke within the prior 3 months[^1]
