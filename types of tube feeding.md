# types of tube feeding

- related: [[enteral feeding in icu]]
- tags: #permanent
- source:

---

- Osmolite: balanced tube feeding
- Jevity: more fiber
- Nepro: Lower electrolytes and low K. Good for CKD/ESRD patients
- Glucerna: calorie dense feeding with low carbohydrate, high K, essentially diabetic diet
- Nutren: calorie dense feeding, less fluid, good for heart failure, pancreatitis patients
