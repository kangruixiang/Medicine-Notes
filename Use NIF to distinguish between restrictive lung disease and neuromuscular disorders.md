# Use NIF to distinguish between restrictive lung disease and neuromuscular disorders

- related: [[Pulmonary Diseases]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- in restrictive lung disease, NIF is normal (muscles are working). The patient can generate the negative pressure, but the decreased lung compliance causes less tidal volume
- in neuromuscular disorder, both NIF and tidal volumes are decreased
