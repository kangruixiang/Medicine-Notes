# LVOT VTI

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]]
- tags: #literature #cardiology #pulmonology

<!-- LVOT VTI calculate SV b:1256167071781-->

- Cardiac output = SV x HR
- SV = CSA (cross sectional area) of LVOT x LVOT VTI
- CSA of LVOT = πr2 = π(d/2)2

Therefore CO = (LVOTd/2)2 x π x LVOT VTI x HR

$$CO=(\frac{LVOTd}{2})^2\times\pi\times LVOT\space VTI\times HR$$
