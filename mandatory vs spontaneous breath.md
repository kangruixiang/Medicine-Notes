# mandatory vs spontaneous breath

- related: [[design features of modern mechanical ventilators]]
- tags: #permanent

---
- spontaneous breath: patient triggered breath
- mandatory breath: the number of breaths each minute that will happen regardless of patient effort. Sometimes patient will trigger a breath with some assistance from the machine. Sometimes when the pause is too long between breaths, the machine will trigger a breath
