# acute mountain sickness

- related: [[Occupational Lung Disease]]
- tags: #literature #pulmonology
---

- low atmospheric pressure at high altitudes
- sx from mild to severe including pulmonary and cerebral edema
- chance of it happening increases the faster a person climbs
- treatment: descend as fast as possible
- acetazolamide can be taken to stimulate breathing
- nifedipine and sildenafil can help with pulmonary edema
- dexamethasone can help with cerebral edema
- portable hyperbaric chambers can be used
