# TXA tranexamic acid reduced mortality in postpartum bleed

- related: [[TXA tranexamic acid uses]]
- tags: #literature #hemeonc
---

<!-- TXA uses in pregnancy bleed -->

In the WOMAN trial, >20,000 patients with postpartum hemorrhage after a vaginal birth or cesarean section were randomized to receive either tranexamic acid or placebo. Death due to bleeding was significantly reduced without a significant increase in complications.[^1][^2]

[^2]: WOMAN Trial Collaborators. Effect of early tranexamic acid administration on mortality, hysterectomy, and other morbidities in women with post-partum haemorrhage (WOMAN): an international, randomised, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial. _Lancet_. 2017;389(10084):2105-2116.
