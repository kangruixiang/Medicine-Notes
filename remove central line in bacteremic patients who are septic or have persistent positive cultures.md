# remove central line in bacteremic patients who are septic or have persistent positive cultures

- related: [[infective endocarditis]], [[Infectious Disease ID]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Line infections can be suggested by following:

- Purulence around line site
- Sepsis, shock requiring pressors
- Positive central line culture >2 hours before peripheral culture
- Persistent positive blood cultures despite antibiotics[^1]

[^1]: https://emcrit.org/ibcc/line/
