## nitroprusside

<!-- IV nitroprusside toxicity: when does it happen. Symptoms. Treatment? b:1384081249538-->

- converted to cyanide via kidney and then thiocyanate in liver
- thus either liver/kidney dysfunction can lead to toxicity
- cyanide toxicity: elevated lactate and AMS
- treatment: IV thiosulfate
