# large PA size on CT can suggest pulmonary hypertension but does not tell severity

- related: [[pulmonary hypertension]]
- tags: #permanent
- source: [[Murray and Nadel Textbook of Respiratory Medicine]]
---

PA diameter over 29 mm or PA to aorta ratio > 1 suggest pulmonary hypertension.

Progressive dilation of PA size is a function of duration of pulmonary hypertension and not severity.
