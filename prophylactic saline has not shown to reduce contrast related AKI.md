# prophylactic saline has not shown to reduce contrast related AKI

- related: [[Nephrology]]
- tags: #permanent
---

The AMACING (A MAastricht Contrast-Induced Nephropathy Guideline) trial found no difference in AKI between patients receiving periprocedural IV saline to no fluids.[^1]

[^1]: https://www.thelancet.com/journals/eclinm/article/PIIS2589-5370(18)30044-0/fulltext
