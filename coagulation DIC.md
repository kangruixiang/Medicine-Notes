## Stages of DIC

- Stage 0: pre DIC
  - not clinically evident
  - coagulation cascade revved up
    - fibinogen increase as result of acute phase reactant
    - platelets increase
    - antithrombin III sufficient
  - similar to pre DM
  - immunothrombosis
  - pro inflammatory state
- Stage 1 DIC: thrombogenic state
  - inflammatory cascade starting to cause problems that are visible
  - overproduction of thrombin => clot formation
  - antithrombin III
  - activation of factor V and VIII
  - activation of platelets
