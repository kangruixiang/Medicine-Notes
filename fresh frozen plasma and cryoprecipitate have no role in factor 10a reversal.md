# fresh frozen plasma and cryoprecipitate have no role in factor 10a reversal

- related: [[anticoagulation medications and reversal]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Fresh frozen plasma is not helpful for reversing factor 10a inhibitors (apixaban, rivaroxaban) and contains very little factor X. Cryoprecipitate also lack factor X.[^1]

[^1]: Tomaselli GF, Mahaffey KW, Cuker A, et al. 2017 ACC Expert Consensus Decision Pathway on management of bleeding in patients on oral anticoagulants: a report of the American College of Cardiology Task Force on Expert Consensus Decision Pathways. _J Am Coll Cardiol_. 2017;70(24):3042-3067. [PubMed](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/29203195)
