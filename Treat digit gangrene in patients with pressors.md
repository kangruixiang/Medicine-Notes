# Treat digit gangrene in patients with pressors

- related: [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #permanent
---
- Elevation of extremities
- Splinting
- Warming with bair hugger
- Remove extra lines, especially arterial lines
- topical nitroglycerin
- Nerve block: stop sympathetic input [^1]

[^1]: Livesey M, Jauregui JJ, Hamaker MC, Pensy RA, Langhammer CG, Eglseder WA. Management of vasopressor induced ischemia. J Orthop. 2020 Oct 16;22:497-502. doi: 10.1016/j.jor.2020.10.012. PMID: 33100742; PMCID: PMC7577194.
