# check factor 10a levels for DOACs, eliquis, apixaban, xarelto, rivaroxaban

- related: [[anticoagulation medications and reversal]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- factor 10a inhibitors (apixaban, rivaroxaban) can have ==normal or abnormal PT/INR==
- checking factor Xa levels can be helpful but not available in acute settings[^1]
