# EGPA treatment includes steroids, meolizumab, immunosuppression

- related: [[eosinophilic granulomatosis with polyangitis EGPA aka churg strauss]]
- tags: #literature #pulmonology
---

- steroid: 1 mg/kg for 4 weeks, then taper over 1 year
- cyclophosphamide
- mepolizumab: 300 mg q4 weeks
