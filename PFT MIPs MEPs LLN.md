# PFT MIPs MEPs LLN

- related: [[PFT and lung functions]]
- tags: #permanent
---

## LLN Calculation

- Male MIP LLN = 62 - (0.15 × age)
- Male MEP LLN = 117 - (0.83 × age)
- Female MIP LLN =62 - (0.50 × age)
- Female MEP LLN = 95 - (0.57 × age)[^1]

## Alternative

- MIP & MEP LLN is 2/3 of average value for given sex [^2]

|     | Male avg (LLN) | Female avg (LLN) |
| --- | -------------- | ---------------- |
| MIP | -100 (67)      | -70 (-47)        |
| MEP | +170 (113)     | +110 (+73)       |

[^1]: Dr. Joseph Gnanaraj
[^2]: [[PFT interpretation guide PGY4?]]
