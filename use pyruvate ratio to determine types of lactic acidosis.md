# use pyruvate ratio to determine types of lactic acidosis

- related: [[lactic acidosis types]]
- tags: #literature #icu
---

<!-- what's lactic to pyruvate ratio? b:116200092666-->

Lactic acidosis is often divided into ==type A==, in which anaerobic metabolism ==drives pyruvate toward lactate==, ==increasing the lactate-pyruvate ratio==, and ==type B==, in which anaerobic metabolism does not play a role and ==lactate-pyruvate ratios are normal==.
