# methods of inpatient pulmonary hygiene

- related: [[pulmonary hygiene therapy]]
- tags: #permanent
---

## Overview

- Start with albuterol nebulizer. Do this at least twice a day and up to every 4 hours. SABA helps with bronchodilator and increase ciliary beating.[^1]
- Ipratropium can be added but has duration of action of 6 hours instead of 4
- If patients use hypertonic saline or dornase alpha at home for CF, these can also be used.
	- [[hypertonic saline does not have any therapeutic benefit in COPD]]
	- [[hypertonic saline for patients with bronchiectasis can improve quality of life and lung function?]]
	- dornase alpha does not show benefit in COPD patients
- Use chest vest immediately after ([[types of pulmonary hygiene devices]] ). The goal is to bronchodilate and then bring up mucous.
- Finish with long acting bronchodilators or inhaled corticosteroids (e.g., pulmicort, brovana)

[^1]: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/956095/
