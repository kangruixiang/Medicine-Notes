BTS 2020 draft:

- MIST2
	- 210, multicenter, double blinded, placebo controlled
	- 210 2005-2008
- [Piccolo](https://www.atsjournals.org/doi/10.1513/AnnalsATS.201407-329OC?url_ver=Z39.88-2003&rfr_id=ori:rid:crossref.org&rfr_dat=cr_pub%20%200pubmed)
	- 107 patients, 8 respiratory centers in Australia, NZ, and UK. Retrospective observational study
	- tpa dornase instilled twice daily up to 6 days
	- tpa followed by flush followed by 40-60 min unclamping, free drainage for 40-60 min
	- followed by dnase, up to 6 doses
- concurrent
	- retrospective cohort study 2010-2015, single institution, no study arm
	- 73 patients enrolled
	- concurrent tPA and DNase administration intrapleurally followed by 60cc saline flush
	- tube clamped for 2 hours before opened to -20 suction
	- therapy given twice daily for maximum of 6 doses
	- Overall success rate of 90.4%
- [management](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/26761711/)
	- 2012-2014,
	- once daily for 3 days
	- tpa instilled followed by 10-25cc flush. Tube clamped for 60 min then suctioned at -20 for 60 min. Followed by DNase
