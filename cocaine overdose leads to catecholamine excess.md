# cocaine overdose leads to catecholamine excess

- related: [[toxicology and toxic ingestions]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- cocaine inhibits catecholamine uptake at synaptic cleft
- symptoms are result of sympathetic stimulation: euphoria, mydriasis, tachycardia, hypertension, hallucination, anxiety, diaphoresis
- result from cocaine overdose can affect multiple organs
	- neuro: lower seizure threshold, stroke
	- cardiac toxicity, MI, cardiomyopathy
	- lung: pneumothorax, bronchospasm
	- msk: hyperthermia, rhabdo[^1]
