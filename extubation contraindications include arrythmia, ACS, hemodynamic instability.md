# extubation contraindications include arrythmia, ACS, hemodynamic instability

- related: [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #permanent
- source:

---

Contraindications for extubation includes high pressor requirements (don't extubate someone who you are afraid to reintubate), tachyarrhythmia or bradyarrhythmia (can affect hemodynamics), active ACS.
