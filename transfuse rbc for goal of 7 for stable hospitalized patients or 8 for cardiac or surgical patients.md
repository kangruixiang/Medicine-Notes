# transfuse rbc for goal of 7 for stable hospitalized patients or 8 for cardiac or surgical patients

- related: [[Hematology]], [[transfusion goals]]
- tags: #permanent

---

For stable, nonbleeding, hospitalized patients, transfusion goal should be 7. For stable, nonbleeding, hospitalized patients who have preexisting coronary artery disease or are undergoing surgery, transfusion goal should be 8 or if patient is symptomatic (chest pain, tachycardia, hypotension). No transfusion goal is set for patients with active ACS ==(needs source)==.
