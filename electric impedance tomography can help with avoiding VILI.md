# electric impedance tomography can help with avoiding VILI

- related: [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- ==electric impedance tomography== (EIT) works by placing sensors around mid chest
- small electrical signals are applied and then received
- air and tissue in the lung returns different impedance
- signals returned then produce cross sectional image of gas volume in the lung
- this can be helpful in preventing VILI: [[ventilator induced lung injury VILI happens from over distention of normal lung?]]

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230710072804.png)

- the four quadrants of the lungs are plotted in the picture with tidal volume for each
- regions of interest (ROI) arranged ventral to dorsal. [^1][^2]

[^1]: Bachmann MC, Morais C, Bugedo G, et al. Electrical impedance tomography in acute respiratory distress syndrome. _Crit Care_. 2018;22(1):263. [PubMed](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/30360753)
[^2]: van der Zee P, Somhorst P, Endeman H, et al. Electrical impedance tomography for positive end-expiratory pressure titration in COVID-19-related acute respiratory distress syndrome. _Am J Respir Crit Care Med_. 2020;202(2):280-284. [PubMed](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32479112)
