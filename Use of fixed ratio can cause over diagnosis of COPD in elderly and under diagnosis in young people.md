# Use of fixed ratio can cause over diagnosis of COPD in elderly and under diagnosis in young people

- related: [[COPD chronic obstructive pulmonary disease]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Elderly patients tend to have less reserve and lung function and have lower FEV1/FVC ratio. Young patients are the opposite. LLN may be better than fixed ratio to diagnose COPD in these populations.[^1]

[^1]: https://goldcopd.org/2023-gold-report-2/
