# MRI with gadolinium contrast in CKD patients has low risk for nephrogenic systemic fibrosis

- related: [[Nephrology]]
- tags: #permanent
- source: https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/full/10.1148/radiol.2020202903
---

Risk of nephrogenic systemic fibrosis in CKD patients who received gadolinium contrast for MRI is very low, around 0.07% overall.
