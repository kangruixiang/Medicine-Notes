# steroid taper for HP hypersensitivity pneumonitis

- related: [[HP hypersensitivity pneumonitis]]
- tags: #literature #pulmonology
---

Prednisone 60 mg orally once a day for 1 to 2 weeks, then tapered over the next 4 to 8 weeks (depending on response) to 20 mg once a day, followed by weekly decrements of 2.5 mg until the drug is stopped.
