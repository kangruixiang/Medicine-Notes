# dupixent dupilumab for severe COPD on triple therapy improves lung function, quality of life, exacerbations

- related: [[COPD chronic obstructive pulmonary disease]]
- tags: #permanent
---

In COPD patients with blood eosinophils > 300 and on triple therapy, Dupixent (dupilumab) every 2 weeks injection improves annual exacerbation rate (RR 0.78, 0.64-0.93), improves SGRQ score, and FEV1 (mean 160 ml).[^1]

[^1]: https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa2303951
