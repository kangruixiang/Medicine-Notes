# use ICS formoterol as initial asthma treatment

- related: [[Asthma reactive airway disease]]
- tags: #permanent
---

ICS containing treatment should be used in all asthma patients. Early treatment is associated with decreased exacerbations, symptoms, improved lung function.[^1][^2]

Formoterol has **fast-onset** kinetics (<5 min) but is long acting and is therefore effective for acute relief.

[^1]: https://ginasthma.org/pocket-guide-for-asthma-management-and-prevention/
[^2]: O’Byrne PM, FitzGerald JM, Bateman ED, et al. Inhaled Combined Budesonide–Formoterol as Needed in Mild Asthma. _New England Journal of Medicine_. 2018;378(20):1865-1876. doi:[10.1056/NEJMoa1715274](https://doi.org/10.1056/NEJMoa1715274)
