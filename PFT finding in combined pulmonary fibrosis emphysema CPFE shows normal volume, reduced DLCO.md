# PFT finding in combined pulmonary fibrosis emphysema CPFE shows normal volume, reduced DLCO

- related: [[combined pulmonary fibrosis emphysema CPFE]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- PFT shows relatively ==normal lung volumes, FVC, and FEV1, and very low DLCO==
- pattern is from ILD increased lung recoil balancing out loss of pulmonary capillary bed
	- both processes can result in low DLCO[^1]
- [[FVC progression for combined pulmonary fibrosis emphysema CPFE may be misleading]]

[^1]: Amariei DE, Dodia N, Deepak J, et al. Combined pulmonary fibrosis and emphysema: pulmonary function testing and a pathophysiology perspective. _Medicina (Kaunas)_. 2019;55(9):580. [PubMed](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/PMID 31509942)
