# bleeding risk may outweigh stroke risk for new onset afib patients in ICU

- related: [[atrial fibrillation]]
- tags: #permanent
---

There is limited evidence for anticoagulation for new onset afib patients in ICU. [^1]

CHADS-VaSC and HAS-BLED have not been validated in ICU patients.[^2]

In ICU patients, bleeding risk may outweigh embolic risk. [^3]

[^1]: https://ccforum.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s13054-021-03684-5

[^2]: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC4470554/

[^3]: https://emcrit.org/ibcc/af/#anticoagulation?
