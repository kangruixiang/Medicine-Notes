# airway clearance and hypertonic saline is key treatment in bronchiectasis

- related: [[chronic bronchiectasis]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- use SABA prior to chest physiotherapy
- followed by LABA/ICS if asthma or LABA
- hypertonic saline: reduced viscosity of mucous
	- add if having trouble with expectorating sputum, persistent sputum, frequent exacerbations ≥ 2 a year
	- can induce bronchospasm. Use SABA before
	- use 3-7% [^1]
