# MRSA risks and infections

- related: [[Infectious Disease ID]], [[MRSA]]
- tag: #literature #pulmonology

---

<!-- empiric MRSA coverage when b:287693956049-->

Empiric MRSA coverage may be included in patients with risk factors for MRSA infection. Risks inculde:

- MRSA nasal colonization
- recent hospitalization
- incarceration
- necrotizing pneumonia or empyema ([[cover for anaerobes for pulmonary abscess and empyema]]) ([[pulmonary cavitary lesions do not need anaerobic coverage vs abscess]])
- recent antibiotic use
- previous MRSA infection
- IVDU
- Do not need MRSA coverage for urosepsis or intra abdominal infection
- Need MRSA coverage for line infection, endocarditis, soft tissue, pneumonia[^1]

[^1]: [IBCC](https://emcrit.org/ibcc/sepsis/#antibiotics)
