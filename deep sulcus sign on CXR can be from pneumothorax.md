# deep sulcus sign on CXR can be from pneumothorax

- related: [[chest imaging]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Air collects in anterior and inferior of the chest when supine. This can cause deepened costophrenic angle (red arrow). Chest xray must be obtained supine.
