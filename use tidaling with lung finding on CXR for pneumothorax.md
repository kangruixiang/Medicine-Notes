# use tidaling with lung finding on CXR for pneumothorax

- related: [[pneumothorax]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---
- tidaling with lung down: large amount of air left
- tidaling with lung up: small amount of air left
- no tidaling with lung down: tube not working, check chest tube
- no tidaling with lung up: complete air evacuation
