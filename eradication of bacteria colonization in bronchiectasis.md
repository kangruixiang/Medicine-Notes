# eradication of bacteria colonization in bronchiectasis

- related: [[chronic bronchiectasis]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Eradication therapy with 2 weeks IV ceftazidime/tobramycin followed by nebulized tobramycin for 3 months for new pseudomonas infections.[^1]

Essentially give 2 weeks of PO or IV abx first, then followed by inhaled tobramycin on/off until eradication at follow up.
