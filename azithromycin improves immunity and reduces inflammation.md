# azithromycin improves immunity and reduces inflammation

- related: [[chronic bronchiectasis]], [[COPD chronic obstructive pulmonary disease]], [[chronic bronchitis]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Azithromycin promotes host defense by stimulating immune and epithelial cells. It also helps to promote inflammation resolution by reducing tissue injury.[^1]

- use azithromycin 250 daily for COPD and three times weekly for chronic bronchitis

[^1]: Parnham MJ, Erakovic Haber V, Giamarellos-Bourboulis EJ, Perletti G, Verleden GM, Vos R. Azithromycin: mechanisms of action and their relevance for clinical applications. _Pharmacol Ther_. 2014;143(2):225-245. doi:10.1016/j.pharmthera.2014.03.003
