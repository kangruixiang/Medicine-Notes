# plasmaphresis have no added benefit for ANCA associated vasculitis

- related: [[Rheumatology]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- [[plasmaphresis is unique treatment to anti GBM]], but does not have improved mortality or ESRD incidence for other ANCA associated vasculitis (GPA, MPA) [^1]

[^1]: Walsh M, Merkel PA, Peh CA, et al; PEXIVAS Investigators. Plasma exchange and glucocorticoids in severe ANCA-associated vasculitis. _N Engl J Med_. 2020;382(7):622-631. [PubMed](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/32053298)
