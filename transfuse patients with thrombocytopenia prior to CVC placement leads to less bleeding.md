# transfuse patients with thrombocytopenia prior to CVC placement leads to less bleeding

- related: [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #permanent
---

In patients with thrombocytopenia (10k-50k), transfusion with 1u platelet prior to CVC placement leads to lower bleeding (4.8% vs 11.9% all bleeding event, and 2.1% vs 4.9% moderate to severe bleeding).[^1]

[^1]: https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa2214322?rss=mostViewed
