# IVC filter may be beneficial in patients with cancer and bleeding

- related: [[Hematology]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- patients with cancer and lower extremity DVT with increased risks for bleeding are more likely to receive IVC filter
- IVC filter increased PE free survival (HR 0.69, 0.64-0.75)
- IVC filter reduced PE in very high risk, high risk, and low risk cancers
- IVC filter does not have increased DVT development.[^1]

[^1]: https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jamanetworkopen/fullarticle/2768568
