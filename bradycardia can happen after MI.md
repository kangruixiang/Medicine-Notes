- acute MI can cause bradycardia due to blood supply interruption

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250719075419.png)

- sinus bradycardia is most common
- inferior MI causes profound vagal tone increase. This is usually worse in first 6 hours and then improves
- atropine can actually be helpful for high vagal tone
