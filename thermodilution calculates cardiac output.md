# thermodilution calculates cardiac output

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]]
- tags: #literature #cardiology
---

- While Fick is gold standard for CO assessment, in ICU thermodilution is usually used
- thermodilution can be done with either pulmonary artery catheter (PAC) or other less invasive techniques
- thermodilution uses room temperature or cold saline with assessment of temperature as the fluid passes by the catheter.

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250723081442.png)

- In low cardiac output state, larger area under neath curve
- high cardiac output: smaller area
