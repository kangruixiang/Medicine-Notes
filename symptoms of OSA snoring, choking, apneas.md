# symptoms of OSA snoring, choking, apneas

- related: [[Sleep and Sleep Disordered Breathing]]
- tags: #permanent
---

symptoms of OSA includes

- snoring, choking, apneas
- fatigue, morning headache, excessive day time sleep
- frequent nocturnal awakenings
- cataplexy points to narcolepsy
- osa patients can have leg movement but no urge to move
