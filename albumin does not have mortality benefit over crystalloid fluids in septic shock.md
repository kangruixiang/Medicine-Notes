# albumin does not have mortality benefit over crystalloid fluids in septic shock

- related: [[fluid management in the ICU]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- SAFE study showed there is no mortality difference between given albumin vs NS in ICU patients
	- subgroup analysis showed higher mortality for trauma patients with head injury for albumin. [^1]
- Another study in 2014 found albumin group had lower heart rate and higher MAP but no mortality difference. [^2]
- ALBICS trial found no difference in outcome of albumin vs LR for patients undergoing cardiac surgery. [^3]
- 2019 meta analysis showed no difference between crystalloid and colloid [^4]
