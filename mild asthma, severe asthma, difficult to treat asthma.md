# mild asthma, severe asthma, difficult to treat asthma

- related: [[Asthma reactive airway disease]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- mild asthma is asthma well controlled on low dose ICS-formoterol
- avoid using the term mild asthma as these patients can also have severe exacerbations. Instead, describe patients as ==well controlled on low or medium dose ICS-LABA==
- severe asthma is asthma that's uncontrolled despite optimal ICS-LABA
- ==difficult to treat asthma== is asthma uncontrolled from other things such as noncompliance, incorrect techniques, comorbidities[^1]

[^1]: https://ginasthma.org/pocket-guide-for-asthma-management-and-prevention/
