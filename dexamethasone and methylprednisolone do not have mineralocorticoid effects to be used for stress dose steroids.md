# dexamethasone and methylprednisolone do not have mineralocorticoid effects to be used for stress dose steroids

- related: [[stress dose steroid]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Do not use dexamethasone or methylprednisolone for septic shock patients as they do not have mineralocorticoid effects.[^1] However, in Covid patients who are receiving dexamethasone and are in septic shock, you do not need to add hydrocortisone.[^2]

[^1]: https://www.uptodate.com/contents/image/print?imageKey=ENDO%2F64138

[^2]: https://www.covid19treatmentguidelines.nih.gov/management/critical-care-for-adults/hemodynamics-for-adults/
