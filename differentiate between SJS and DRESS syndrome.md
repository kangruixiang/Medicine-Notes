# differentiate between SJS and DRESS syndrome

- related: [[Dermatology]]
- tags: #permanent #icu
---

<!-- how to differentiate between SJS and DRESS? -->

SJS and DRESS have similar medication causes

- [[steven johnson syndrome causes include seizure medications]]
- [[DRESS syndrome causes]]

They can differ in their location of skin findings and labs:

- Eventually the patients develop striking ==facial edema and redness== (SJS/TEN does not have facial edema), a hallmark of this condition. Oral mucosal involvement is common but is less severe than SJS/TEN or EM major.
- Eosinophilia is more common in DRESS
