## Highlights

- Bronchoscopy with lavage can be performed with platelet counts >20 000 per μL [p.1](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DZYIQEFY?page=1&annotation=T7T2X3V3)
- TBLB is helpful in lung transplant recipients when rejection is a possibility. [p.2](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DZYIQEFY?page=2&annotation=VI4MCUTZ)
- No more than 5 mg midazolam should be initially drawn up [p.2](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DZYIQEFY?page=2&annotation=8WGQWEI5)
- should only be used when administered by practitioners formally trained in its administration (eg, anaesthetists) [p.2](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DZYIQEFY?page=2&annotation=NLCDWRG3)
	- _don't use propofol_
- Nebulisation is not recommended as a technique for delivering lidocaine to the airways. [p.2](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DZYIQEFY?page=2&annotation=6UP7CFNV)
- 1% lidocaine solution should be used for spray-as-you-go administration. [p.2](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DZYIQEFY?page=2&annotation=MMQ5QTD4)
- TBLB is recommended for the diagnosis of stage II–IV sarcoidosis. [p.2](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DZYIQEFY?page=2&annotation=CYRKB5MI)
- diffuse interstitial lung disease (ILD), five to six TBLBs should be taken from the same lung. [p.2](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DZYIQEFY?page=2&annotation=7GZS29XZ)
