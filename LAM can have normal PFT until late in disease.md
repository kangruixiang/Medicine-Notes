# LAM can have normal PFT until late in disease

- related: [[lymphangioleiomyomatosis LAM]]
- tags: #literature #pulmonology
---

<!-- LAM PFT finding b:805999334751-->

The majority of patients with LAM have ==normal physiologic testing==, with the following in order of frequency: reduction in DLCO, obstructive pattern, and restrictive or mixed obstructive and restrictive pattern. Progressive decline in lung function occurs as ==dysregulated smooth musclelike cells (LAM cells) infiltrate the lung==, disrupting lymphatics and leading to parenchymal destruction and ==cyst formation==.[^1]
