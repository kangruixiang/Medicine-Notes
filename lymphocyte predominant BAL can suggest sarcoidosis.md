# lymphocyte predominant BAL can suggest sarcoidosis

- related: [[pulmonary and extrapulmonary sarcoidosis]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---

- obtain BAL when doing biopsy for EBUS lymph nodes
- lymphocyte by itself is not specific ([[lymphocytic BAL points to inflammatory etiologies]])
- positive biopsy, CD4:CD8 > 4, lymphocyte predominant BAL is predicative of sarcoid
- low CD4:CD8 ratio could suggest [[check point inhibitor pneumonitis]][^1]

[^1]: [[OPPM sarcoidosis]]
