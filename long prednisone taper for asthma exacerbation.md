# long prednisone taper for asthma exacerbation

- related: [[Endocrinology]], [[Asthma reactive airway disease]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Use 2 week taper for severe asthma exacerbation. COPD patients do not generally need long prednisone taper:

- Total 16 days, use prednisone 10 mg, total 40 tablets
	- 40 mg x 4 days
	- 30 mg x 4 days
	- 20 mg x 4 days
	- 10 mg x 4 days
