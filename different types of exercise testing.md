# different types of exercise testing

- related: [[cardiopulmonary exercise test CPET]]

## Submaximal test

- 6 minute walk
- incremental shuttle walk test: walk between cones, faster and faster until short of breath[^1]
- stair climb

## Maximal test

- treadmill
- cycle ergometer
