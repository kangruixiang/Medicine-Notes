# bronchiectasis exacerbation is defined by worsening symptoms for 48 hours needing treatment

- related: [[chronic bronchiectasis]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Bronchiectasis has distinct feature of exacerbation characterized by several factors:

- worsening cough, sputum production
- symptoms continued for 48 hours
- determination by clinician that a change in treatment is needed.[^1]
 - aim for 10-14 days abx duration
 - dual IV abx for pseudomonas can be considered (ceftazidime and tobramycin) ([[eradication of bacteria colonization in bronchiectasis]])

[^1]: [[Bronchiectasis A Clinical Review]]
