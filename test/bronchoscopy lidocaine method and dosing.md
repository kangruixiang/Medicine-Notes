# bronchoscopy lidocaine method and dosing

- related: [[Pulmonology]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---
- Total lidocaine dose can be given is 8.2 mg/kg
	- approximately 29 cc of a 2% solution for a 70 kg patient [^1][^2]
	- 1 cc of lidocaine is 20 mg for 2% solution
- use lidocaine 2% solution for atomizer and Jackson forceps
- for nebulizing: 5 cc nebulize with face mask[^3]
- atomizer: draw up 4-5 cc total
	- give each nostril 1 cc twice for total of 4 cc
	- give back of throat 1 cc
	- this would give total of 5 cc lidocaine, or 100 mg
- use lidocaine 2% jelly for qtips
- for cords and carina: use 10 cc syringe
	- use 3 cc x 2 both for cords
	- use 3 cc x 2 at the carina
