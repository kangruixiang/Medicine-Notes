# bronchiolitis with air trapping can have appearance of decreased lung density on expiration

- related: [[respiratory bronchiolitis]]
- tags: #literature
- source: [[An Algorithmic Approach to Lung Disease]]

---

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20221204143043.png)

- on expiration, normal lung becomes denser and whiter. Abnormal lung remains dark.

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20241015101040.png)Chest CT (axial views): respiratory bronchiolitis-associated interstitial lung disease (RB-ILD).

CT findings in RB-ILD commonly consist of ==patchy ground glass opacities==, ==centrilobular nodules==, and ==air trapping==. [^1]
