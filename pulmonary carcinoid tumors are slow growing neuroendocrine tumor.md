==Hamartomas== represent up to 10% of larger solid nodules found in the lung. They may grow slowly, contain calcification (at times in a popcorn pattern), and have focal areas of fat visible on CT imaging. Histology is heterogeneous, often showing ==cartilage and fat== in resection specimens (Figure 6). Imaging and pathology in the presented case do not show these features (choice D is incorrect).[^1]
