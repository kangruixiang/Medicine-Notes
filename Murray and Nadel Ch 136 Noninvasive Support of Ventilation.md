## Home Noninvasive Ventilation

- home NIV is indicated in hypoventilation during sleep

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230816100515.png)

- AHRF: acute hypercapnic respiratory failure
- CPAP or BPAP have similar data for mortality outcome
- Start with CPAP first, unless patient has acute hypercapnic respiratory failure
