# chylothorax is elevated TG with low cholesterol

- related: [[pleural effusion]]
- tags: #permanent #literature #pulmonology
---

<!-- diagnosis between chylothorax and pseudochylothorax b:901981444095-->

- chylothorax typically have triglycerides over 110 and cholesterol less than 200[^1]
- pseudochylothorax: [[rheumatoid effusions have elevated cholesterol, high LDH, low glucose]]
- [[lymphangioleiomyomatosis has cystic lesions with chylous effusions on chest imaging]]
- Lymphangiogram can be used to diagnose chylothorax but can often also treat it
