# COPD patients should get pneumonia vaccine

- related: [[COPD chronic obstructive pulmonary disease]]
- tags: #permanent
- source: [[COPD 2023 GOLD Report]]
---

The available pneumonia vaccine are as follows:

- PCV15 followed by PPSV23
- PCV20, one dose

This can be also found on CDC website:

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230212213019.png)

PCV13 is no longer the recommended vaccine to get for adults:
