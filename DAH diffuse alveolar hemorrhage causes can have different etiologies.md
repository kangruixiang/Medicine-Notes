<!-- DAH causes -->

DAH is a rare clinical syndrome characterized by acute onset hypoxemic respiratory failure caused by progressive diffuse alveolar infiltrates and is the most likely cause of acute hypoxemic respiratory failure in this patient. ==DAH== is a known complication in patients with systemic inflammatory disorders or hematologic malignancies and is characterized into four categories: ==immune associated==, ==congestive heart failure==, ==idiopathic==, and ==miscellaneous==. Miscellaneous causes of DAH include ==infections==, ==malignancies==, ==drugs==, and ==hematopoietic stem cell transplants==.[^1][^2]

- autoimmune
	- [[lupus with DAH]]
	- [[Goodpasture syndrome anti-GBM can develop DAH]]
- malignancies
- infections
- stem cell transplant
	- [[DAH can develop following stem cell transplant]]
- heart failure
- drugs
- idiopathic
	- [[idiopathic pulmonary hemosiderosis IPH is idiopathic DAH]]
