# CF cystic fibrosis

- related: [[Pulmonary Diseases]]
- tags: #pulmonology

---

## Diagnosis

- [[cystic fibrosis diagnosis]]

## Treatment

- [[cystic fibrosis treat with Trikafta over mono or dual therapy]]
- Maybe aztreonam
- Start azithromycin after 2nd exacerbation
- Stool elastase for pancreatic insufficency. If low then Creon
- Once a year labs usually
	- IgE and eos to screen for ABPA
	- CMP for CF related liver and trikafta
	- CBC: for anemia
	- Immunoglobulins: immunodeficiency
	- Can have hyponatremia and Chloride because they can sweat with exercise
- Supplement with oral NaCl tablets

## Complications

- CF patients are at increased risks for ABPA: [[diagnosis of ABPA includes eosinophil count, antibody level, and CT image findings]].
- [[CF patients are at increased risk for NTM MAC infections]]
