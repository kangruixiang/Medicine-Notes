# IBCC - severe community acquired pneumonia

- related: [[community acquired pneumonia]]
- tags: #literature
- source: [IBCC](https://emcrit.org/ibcc/cap/#labs_to_obtain_after_diagnosing_pneumonia)

---

- pneumonia is community acquired when within first 48 hours of admission
- ddx
	- aspiration pneumonitis: CXR clears up in 2 days, procalcitonin low
	- COPD: can have both pna and COPD. Will need to treat both. Procal usually low
- sputum
	- needs < 10 squamous epithelial cells and > 25 WBC to be useful
	- multiple different bacteria usually means contaminates
