# treatment of cystic fibrosis exacerbation

- related: [[CF cystic fibrosis]]
- tags: #permanent
- source:
---

- obtain CF sputum culture
- start antibiotics based on previous culture
	- resistant organism does not mean cannot use that antibiotic
	- reserve cipro for outpatient treatment
	- total 14 to 21 days therapy if serious disease
- obtain FEV1 on day 1 and day 7 and adjust antibiotic timing based off of result
- vest therapy: 6 times per day
- order of respiratory therapy:
	- start with albuterol to open up the airway
	- next use hypertonic saline and pulmozyme/dornase alpha to break up thick secretions
	- then use vest therapy
	- last give inhaelers (ICS/LABA)
