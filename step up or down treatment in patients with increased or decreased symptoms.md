# step up or down treatment in patients with increased or decreased symptoms

- related: [[Asthma reactive airway disease]]
- tags: #permanent
- source: [[2022 GINA Report]]
---

For patients with worsening symptoms, step up therapy and repeat PFT in 3 months. If a patient has well controlled symptoms, can stp down treatment
