# anaerobic threshold is point of anaerobic metabolism in CPET

- related: [[cardiopulmonary exercise test CPET]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---
- it is the point at which anaerobic metabolism takes over
- CO2 production increases more rapidly than O2 consumption from anaerobic metabolism[^1]

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20240708081043.png)

- this is different from ventilatory threshold ([[ventilatory threshold is point of max exercise capacity]]).

[^1]: [[Principles of Exercise Testing and Interpretation]]
