## Conditions and Challenges

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016132140.png)

- up to 40% CHF, 15-20% NMD, up to 50% COPD, most of OSA

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016132235.png)

- bicarb predicts in pts with elevated BMI

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016132339.png)

- ataxic breathing
- central pauses can become as long as 2-3 min with agonal breathing
- often in opioid users

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016132431.png)

## Neuromuscular disorders

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016132549.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016132627.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016132649.png)

- laying flat can show dramatic drops

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016132711.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016132914.png)

- OSA: quick drops and recovery
- COPD: sustained desat with CO2 go up
- Combined: dramatic changes in both CO2 and desat

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133000.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133011.png)

- not every needs NIV
- CPAP effective in a lot of cases, will improve CO2 but not as fast as Bipap.
- CPAP can treat OHS with elevated CO2 if stable

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133108.png)

- survival in NIV treated groups is higher with NIV when there is overlap

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133141.png)

- normocapneic patient doesn't receive as much benefit from bipap

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133310.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133501.png)

- ALS pts with severe bulbar symptoms don't benefit as much

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133535.png)

- reduction in mortality less so in eucapnic morbid obesity

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133659.png)

- correcting CO2 maximally even shows benefit

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133739.png)

## Monitoring

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133853.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016133905.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016134001.png)

- mean co2 and tCO2 can be quite different between different conditions

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016134106.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016134126.png)

- pressure based on TV
- intestinal obstruction: should probably not do NIV if they don't have NG in place

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016134229.png)

- 6-8 cc/kg + patient's dead space (around 150cc) should be upper limit target of TV

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016134313.png)

- BMI over 30: start EPAP of 8 or 10 to overcome upper airway obstruction
- look at flow loop to make sure there's no obstruction on inspiratory flow loop

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016134436.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016134540.png)

- no dramatic change from NIV with AVAPS
- Drift: lower and lower PS to assure tidal volume with AVAPS. Trilogy/AVAPS bug that can cause recurring admission

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016134905.png)

- AVAPS gets patient to PEEP faster than trained technicians at night in NMD, COPD, OHS

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016134937.png)

- RLD and OHS: really extend Timin and Timax for diaphragmatic support. Get TiMax to 15

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016135314.png)

- Ti min prevents premature cycle
- Timax prevents inspiration leaks?

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016135351.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016135502.png)

- once pressure target exceeded, respiratory rate then is increased

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016135548.png)

- new technology is better at adjusting pressure to not exceed pressure target with mask leaks

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20251016135619.png)

- send pts home with Astral devices, IVAPS
