# rheumatoid effusions have elevated cholesterol, high LDH, low glucose

- related: [[pleural effusion]]
- tags: #permanent
---

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20231130063749.png)

- rheumatoid effusions sometimes are known as pseudochylothorax
- they can have milky green appearance
- TG is usually low with cholesterol high. Contrast this with chylorhorax: [[chylothorax is elevated TG with low cholesterol]][^1]

[^1]: Groner LK, Green DB, Weisman SV, Legasto AC, Toy D, Gruden JF, Escalon JG. Thoracic Manifestations of Rheumatoid Arthritis. Radiographics. 2021 Jan-Feb;41(1):32-55. doi: 10.1148/rg.2021200091. PMID: 33411607.
