![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230829121107.png)

### MTP

- MTP: original def 10u/24hrs
- >3u prbc in 1 hr
- in the past: BP, UOP
- today: prevent coagulopathy, AKI, ARDS, consequence of reperfusion

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230829121713.png)

- new: RH+ whole blood for pregnant women as well
- leukocyte reduced
- low titer O blood: low anti A / B levels
- whole blood: 500 cc

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230829122147.png)

- 24h consumption less
- uses more upfront

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230829122213.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230829122430.png)

- blunt/penetrating trauma are very different
