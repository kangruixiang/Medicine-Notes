# pathogenesis of chronic bronchiectasis from infection

- related: [[chronic bronchiectasis]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---

- infection leads to neutrophil inflammation and ==elevated proteases== ([[use of brensocatib reduces exacerbation in bronchiectasis]])
- inflammation leads to airway destruction aka bronchiectasis
- bronchiectasis leads to abnormal mucous clearance
- abnormal mucous clearance leads to bacterial colonization
