# avoid pulmozyme or dornase alfa in non-cystic fibrosis CF bronchiectasis

- related: bronchiectasis [[chronic bronchiectasis]], [[CF cystic fibrosis]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---

- pulmozyme not useful in non-CF bronchiectasis patients and can actually lead to increased exacerbation and decreased FEV1 [^1], [^2]

[^1]: [[Bronchiectasis Lecture by Jennifer Krall?]]
[^2]: O'Donnell A.E., Barker A.F., Ilowite J.S., et. al.: Treatment of idiopathic bronchiectasis with aerosolized recombinant human DNase I. rhDNase Study Group. Chest 1998; 113: pp. 1329-1334.
