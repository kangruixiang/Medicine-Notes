# goodpasture differs from GPA with lack of constitutional symptoms

- related: [[goodpasture syndrome anti GBM]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- GPA and Goodpasture both have pulmonary and renal involvement
- GPA also has sinusitis and skin involvement
- lack of constitutional symptoms (fever, weight loss, joint pain, fatigue) makes goodpasture more likely[^1]
- Very high ESR is unusual in anti-GBM
