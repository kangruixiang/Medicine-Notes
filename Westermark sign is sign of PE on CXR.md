# Westermark sign is sign of PE on CXR

- related: [[chest imaging]]
- tags: #permanent
---

There are two findings:

- enlargement of pulmonary artery
- ==oligemia:== collapse of distal pulmonary arteries due to hypoxic vasoconstriction distal to obstruction leading to loss of blood volume and increased lucency. [^1]
