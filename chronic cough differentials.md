# chronic cough differentials

- related: [[Pulmonary Diseases]]
- tags: #permanent
- source: [[2022 GINA Report]]
---

Differentials for chronic cough includes chronic upper airway cough syndrome (post nasal drip), GERD, ACE induced cough, vocal cord dysfunction ([[vocal cord dysfunction can be an asthma mimic]]), eosinophilic bronchitis, cough variant asthma.
