# balloon pump should be 2-3 cm away from subclavian artery

- related: [[cardiac supportive devices]]
- tags: #permanent #icu
---

<!-- where should balloon pump sit location wise and what are complications of misplacement? b:488612605352-->

Balloon should be located just below left subclavian artery, at the level of AP window on CXR

- too high: occlude CNS vessels
- too low: occlude abdominal vessels
- either case can lead to organ ischemia [^1]
