# exercise is normally cardiac limited

- related: [[cardiopulmonary exercise test CPET]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20240708074445.png)

- during exercise, the amount of O2 needed by muscles and CO2 produced increases
- This results in physiologic responses from heart and lungs
- the heart: increase heart rate, peripheral vasodilation, stroke volume to increase cardiac output
- the lung: increase tidal volume, respiratory rate to increase ventilation[^1]

[^1]: [[Principles of Exercise Testing and Interpretation]]
