# increase sensitivity of pacemakers by lowering mV

- related: [[cardiac pacemaker]]
- tags: #permanent
---

<!-- pacemaker sensitivity, what does increase or decrease sensitivity mean? b:280709294365-->

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250719081342.png)

- pacemaker will see based on how high the "brick-wall" is
- 0.5: will see QRS but will also see T wave
- increase sensitivity by lowering number on pacemaker
- increase sensitivity leads to pacemaker sense more noise and less pacing
- decrease sensitivity by increasing the mVolts
- decrease sensitivity leads to pacemaker sense less and more pacing
- atrium usually need 0.5 mV sensing
- ventricles need 2.0 mV sensing

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250719081155.png)
