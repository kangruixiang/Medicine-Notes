# straight edge sign differentiates between NSIP and IPF

- related: [[NSIP nonspecific interstitial pneumonitis]]
- tags: #literature #pulmonology
---

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20241214150843.png)

- NSIP straight edge sign is confined to lower lung fields
- IPF lacks straight edge sign (fibrosis creeps up the lateral edges of the lungs)[^1]

[^1]: https://emcrit.org/ibcc/nsip/
