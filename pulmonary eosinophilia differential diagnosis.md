# pulmonary eosinophilia differential diagnosis

- related: [[Pulmonary Diseases]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Causes of pulmonary eosinophilia are broad:

- lung irradiation
- parasitic infections
- fungal infections (coccidioides have been associated)
- [[acute and chronic eosinophilic pneumonia AEP and CEP]]
- [[diagnosis of ABPA includes eosinophil count, antibody level, and CT image findings]]
- [[eosinophilic granulomatosis with polyangitis EGPA aka churg strauss]][^1]
