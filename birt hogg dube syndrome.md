==1.Birt Hogg Dube==

==2.autosomal dominant== inheritance; characterized by ==2.cystic lung disease::type of lung disease== (90% by adulthood), ==2.pneumothoraces== (≥25%), ==2.skin lesions (fibrofolliculomas)::skin findings==, and propensity for ==2.renal tumors (particularly chromophobe renal cell carcinoma and oncocytoma)::another organ finding==. Surgical lung biopsy will demonstrate emphysema-like lesions without diagnostic features. However, skin biopsy will likely be diagnostic and demonstrate ==2.fibrofolliculomas==. Genetic testing is not necessary for diagnosis, but can be helpful in some cases.[^1]
