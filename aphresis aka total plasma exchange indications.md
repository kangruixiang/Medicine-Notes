![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250814093442.png)

- aphresis is AKA total plasma exchange
- can add back ADAMTS13 in TTP
- AIDP/GBS: no significant differences in outcome in IVIG vs plasmapheresis. IVIG is less invasive so first line
- anti-GBM: usually when having respiratory failure from alveolar failure
- liver failure: not really used

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250814093657.png)

- TPE alters pretty much all biomarkers
- can remove volume but not free water
- can remove medications[^1]
