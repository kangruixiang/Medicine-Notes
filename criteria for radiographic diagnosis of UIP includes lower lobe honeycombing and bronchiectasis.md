# criteria for radiographic diagnosis of UIP includes lower lobe honeycombing and bronchiectasis

- related: [[ILD Interstitial Lung Disease or Diffuse Parenchymal Lung Disease]]
- tags: #permanent
---

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20221203153349.png)

- peripheral and subpleural reticulation
	- worse in costophrenic angle
	- has upper lobe involvement
	- heterogenous
	- crosses fissures
- traction bronchiectasis or honeycombing

---

Usual interstitial pneumonia is the pathologic manifestation of idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis, but can also be seen as an end-stage finding with chronic hypersensitivity pneumonitis, drug toxicity, and rarely sarcoidosis. It consists of primarily peripheral and basal reticular opacities with honeycombing.
