# oxygen delivery is dependent on cardiac output and oxygen content

- related: [[pathophysiology of hypoxia and hypoxemia]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- Oxygen delivery is dependent on cardiac output and $O_2$ content[^2]

$$
O_2\space delivery = CO\times\space O_2\space Content
$$

- Oxygen content is the difference between arterial and venous oxygen. The equation can be rewritten as follows[^1]:

$$
V_{O_2}\space = CO\times\space (C_{aO_2}- C_{vO_2})
$$

- Oxygen content is also dependent on several factors:

$$
O_2\space Content = 1.39\times Hgb\times \%Sat +0.003\times P_aO_2 
$$

[^1]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fick_principle
[^2]: [[cardiopulmonary exercise test CPET]]
