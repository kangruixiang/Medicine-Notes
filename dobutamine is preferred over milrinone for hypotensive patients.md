# dobutamine is preferred over milrinone for hypotensive patients

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]]
- tags: #permanent
---

dobutamine has shorter acting effects. It also is better in patients with renal dysfunction.

Milrinone has more vasodilatory effect and less chronotropic effect and may be better for arrythmia
