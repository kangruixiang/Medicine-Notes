# tandem heart is transseptal LV ECMO

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]]
- tags: #literature #cardiology
---

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250721082239.png)

- like ecmo but transseptal LA drainage, FA return circuit
- drains from LA, out of body, then back to femoral artery
- unloads LV if it can't pump against afterload
- AKA LA-VA, left atrial va ecmo[^1]

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250807133302.png)

- don't have this controller anymore. Rare to do now. [^2]
