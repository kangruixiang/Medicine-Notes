# STEMI criteria

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]]
- tags: #literature #cardiology
---

<!-- STEMI criteria b:889806907845-->

- ST elevation > **1mm in 2 or more contiguous leads**
	- J point elevation for 2 mm in V2 or V3
	- 2.5 mm in men less than 40
	- 1.5 mm in all women
- OR new LBBB ([[bundle branch block EKG findings]])
