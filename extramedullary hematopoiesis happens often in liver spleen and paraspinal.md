# extramedullary hematopoiesis happens often in liver spleen and paraspinal

- related: [[Hematology]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- extramedullary hematopoiesis is production of blood outside marrow
- common location includes liver, spleen
- occasional will have paraspinal involvement in the chest
- CT shows hypo vascular soft tissue mass and fat attenuation without calcium
- biopsy can result in bleeding and should be approached with caution. [^1] [^2]
