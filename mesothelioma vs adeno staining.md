# mesothelioma vs adeno staining

- related: [[mesothelioma]]
- tags: #literature #pulmonology
---

<!-- mesothelioma vs adenocarcinoma staining b:1488901928495-->

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20241015103029.png)

Immunohistochemical staining and molecular testing help to confirm a diagnosis. Positive staining for calretinin, Wilms tumor-1 gene product, and cytokeratin 5/6 is associated with mesothelioma, while tumors that show immunoreactivity for TTF-1, MOC-31, and Claudin-4 staining are more consistent with adenocarcinoma.[^1]
