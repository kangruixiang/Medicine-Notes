# lymphangioleiomyomatosis has cystic lesions with chylous effusions on chest imaging

- related: [[lymphangioleiomyomatosis LAM]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology

---

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20231222194654.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20221204143335.png)

- small cysts with very thin walls
- usually diffusely distributed with otherwise normal lung parenchyma[^1]
- small arteries at periphery of cysts indicates perilobular location in relation to lymphatics
- pleural effusion usually can have white appearance and can be chylous ([[chylothorax is elevated TG with low cholesterol]])[^2]

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20231222195248.png)

- sometimes can see ground glass opacities from interstitial edema from lymphatic obstruction
