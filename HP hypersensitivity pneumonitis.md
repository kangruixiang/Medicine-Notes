# HP hypersensitivity pneumonitis

- related: [[ILD Interstitial Lung Disease or Diffuse Parenchymal Lung Disease]]
- tags: #literature
---

- [[HP hypersensitivity pneumonitis is divided into fibrotic and nonfibrotic types]]

## Image findings

- [[image findings in hypersensitivity pneumonitis include GGO, centrilobular micronodules, and mosaicism]]
- air trapping differentiates between fibrotic NSIP and fibrotic HP
	- NSIP has no air trapping. Use HRCT
- fibrotic HP usually will also have LAD

## Diagnosis

- [[diagnosis of HP is in imaging and BAL with high lymphocyte count]]

## Disease associations

- [[MAC infection can induce hypersensitivity pneumonitis in patients with exposure to hot tube water]]
- [[mulch pneumonitis is hypersensitivity in CGD patients]]
- pool, hot tub, mold, cheese, beer

## Treatment

- [[steroid taper for HP hypersensitivity pneumonitis]]
- Pred first line and cellcept next to taper off pred. ritux is salvage therapy
