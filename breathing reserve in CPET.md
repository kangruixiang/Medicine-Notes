# breathing reserve in CPET

- related: [[cardiopulmonary exercise test CPET]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---

- normal breathing reserve

$$BR = MVV - V_emax$$

- $V_e$: minute ventilation
- $MVV$: maximum voluntary ventilation, either $FEV_1 \times 40$ or 12 seconds rapid deep breathing, x 5
