# common variable immunodeficiency disorder CVID

- related: [[Pulmonary Diseases]]
- tags: #literature
---

## Pathogenesis

- impaired B cell differentation

## Patient population

- often has pneumonia covered by childhood vaccines. Look for ==encapsulated organisms== (H flu, Strep pneumo)
	- rare ones include NTM, Cryptococcus
	- complement deficiency will also have encapsulated organisms but without bronchiectasis or lung diseases
- hepatosplenomegaly
- bilateral small lung nodules
- bronchiectasis
- mixed obstructive/restrictive PFT

## Management

- Check IgM, IgG, IgA
