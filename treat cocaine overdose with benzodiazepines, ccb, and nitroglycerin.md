# treat cocaine overdose with benzodiazepines, ccb, and nitroglycerin

- related: [[toxicology and toxic ingestions]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- avoid beta blockers due to unopposed alpha effect
	- carvedilol and labetalol are ok
- use high dose benzo as first line agent to decrease agitation, seizure, HTN, chest pain
- nitro and CCB can be used in conjunction for HTN and tachyardia[^1]
