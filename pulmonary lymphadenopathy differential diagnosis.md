# pulmonary lymphadenopathy differential diagnosis

- related: [[Pulmonary Diseases]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---

- Autoimmune: [[pulmonary and extrapulmonary sarcoidosis]]
	- [[pulmonary sarcoidosis findings include mediastinal lymphadenopathy and perilymphatic nodules]]
- Calcified lymph nodes: think about sarcoid and tuberculosis
- Cancer: HL, NHL, bronchogenic cancer
- Infections: histo
- Occupational lung disease: chronic berrylium exposure, silicosis
