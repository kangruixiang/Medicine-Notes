# elevated shock index can suggest shock

- related: [[Management of Life-Threatening Asthma]], [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #literature
- source: [pubmed](https://pubmed-ncbi-nlm-nih-gov.wake.idm.oclc.org/35218742/), [sciencedirect](https://www-sciencedirect-com.wake.idm.oclc.org/science/article/pii/S0039606016304858)
