# station 4R is bordered by SVC, azygos, and trachea

- related: [[Chest lymph nodes and where to find them]]
- tags: #permanent
---

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230708072604.png)

- anterior border: SVC
- posterior and medial border: trachea
- lower border: azygos
- distinguishing 4R and 10R is important for staging ([[azygos vein and pulmonary artery distinguish hilar and mediastinal lymph nodes]])

## EBUS

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230708075843.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230729000205.png)

- on EBUS, navigate to right above carina or immediately in right mainstem and look 1-2 o'clock
- slowly come up and pan from 11-3 o'clock as you do
- SVC will appear below 4R (farther away from scope)
- azygos will appear to left of 4R (left is inferior)
