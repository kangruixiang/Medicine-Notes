# sleep latency is how long it takes to fall asleep

- related: [[Sleep and Sleep Disordered Breathing]]
- tags: #literature
---
- normally 10-20 minutes
- less than 8
	- sleep deprivation
	- going to bed later
	- poor sleep from sleep disordered breathing
	- substance: alcohol
- longer
	- could be going to bed earlier
	- medical problems: chronic pain
