## Treatment

- [[inhaled corticosteroid equivalents]]
- [[step up or down treatment in patients with increased or decreased symptoms]]
- [[asthma step up therapies]]
- [[SABA is not recommended for treatment of mild asthma]]
- [[use ICS formoterol as initial asthma treatment]]
- [[add LAMA to asthma treatment improves lung function but not symptoms]]
- [[Moores triad of asthma management]]
- [[acute asthma exacerbation]]
- [[long prednisone taper for asthma exacerbation]]
- [[continue normal asthma management except ICS LABA in pregnant patients]]
- [[treating asthma with bronchodilator acutely does not treat inflammation]]
- [[use bronchial thermoplasty to treat severe asthma]]
- [[dupilumab dupixent is used to treat oral corticosteroid dependent asthma]]
- [[dupilumab dupixent can have transient elevation in blood eosinophils]]
- [[start allergen immunotherapy for controlled asthma patients]]
