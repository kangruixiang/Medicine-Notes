# steroid equivalents

- related: [[Endocrinology]]
- #permanent
---

## Hydrocortisone to prednisone: 4 to 1

- hydrocortisone 200 mg a day is equivalent to prednisone 50 mg daily

## Decadron to prednisone: 1 to 6.5

- decadron 6 == prednisone 40
- decadron 20 == prednisone 130[^1]

## Decadron to methylprednisolone: 1 to 5

- decadron 20 == methylprednisolone 100

## Decadron to hydrocortisone: 1 to 25

- decadron 20 == hydrocortisone 500[^2]
