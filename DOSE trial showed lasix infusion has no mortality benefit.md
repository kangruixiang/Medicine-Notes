# DOSE trial showed lasix infusion has no mortality benefit

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- low dose bolus or infusion: home diuretic converted to IV equivalent
- high dose bolus or infusion: 2.5x home diuretic converted to IV
- comparing bolus vs infusion, there's no mortality differences, no difference in symptoms, change in Cr
- infusion at higher dose has trend towards faster improvement in symptoms, greater diuresis, but worse renal function.[^1]

[^1]: https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/nejmoa1005419
