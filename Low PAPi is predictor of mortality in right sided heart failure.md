# Low PAPi is predictor of mortality in right sided heart failure

- related: [[cardiac hemodynamics and pressures]]
- tags: #permanent
---

PAPi is often used as predictor of RV failure in inferior MI and as monitoring for left assist ventricular device placement.

It's also associated with mortality in pulmonary arterial hypertension.[^2]

A low PAPi (closer to 1) is associated with increased all cause mortality and major cardiac event.[^1]

[[PAPi is ratio of pulmonary artery pulse pressure to right atrial pressure]]
