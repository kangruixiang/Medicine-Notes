# use beta hydroxybutyrate, ketones, and not pH to diagnose DKA

- related: [[DKA diabetic ketoacidosis and HHS]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- patients can have DKA and have normal pH and bicarb
	- if they have vomiting, they can also have concomitant metabolic alkalosis
- using urine ketones and elevation in beta hydroxybutyrate is a better way to assess DKA.[^1]

[^1]: https://emcrit.org/ibcc/dka/
