# PVARP post ventricular atrial refractory period avoids pacer induced arrythmia

- related: [[cardiac pacemaker]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- pacer induced arrythmia happens when A sensing lead senses residual QRS or retrograde P wave
- this leads to another triggering of V lead
- PVARP or post ventricular atrial refractory period can avoid that by setting a refractory period of atrial sensing after ventricular pacing
- this leads to max heart rate 120 (PR trigger + refractory period)
