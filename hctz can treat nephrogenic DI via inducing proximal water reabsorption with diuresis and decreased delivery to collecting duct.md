# hctz can treat nephrogenic DI via inducing proximal water reabsorption with diuresis and decreased delivery to collecting duct

- related: [[diabetes insipidus]]
- tags: #permanent
- source: [uptodate](https://www.uptodate.com/contents/treatment-of-nephrogenic-diabetes-insipidus#H5)

---

HCTZ induces mild hyponatremia, resulting in decreased water delivery to distal site and lower urine output
