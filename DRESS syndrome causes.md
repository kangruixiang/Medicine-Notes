# DRESS syndrome causes

- related: [[Dermatology]]
- tags: #literature #icu
---

==Drug hypersensitivity syndrome== (DHS) and drug reaction with eosinophilia and systemic symptoms ==(DRESS)== are synonymous and represent another severe life-threatening medication reaction. The most common culprit medications include ==sulfonamide antibiotics, allopurinol, and anticonvulsants==, but many more medications have been implicated (Table 30).[^1][^2][^3][^4][^5]

- sulfonamide antibiotics
- allopurinol
- anticonvulsants
- very similar to SJS causes ([[steven johnson syndrome causes include seizure medications]])
