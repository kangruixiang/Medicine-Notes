# use maintenance wakefulness test MWT to test how sleepy during day

- related: [[Sleep and Sleep Disordered Breathing]]
- tags: #literature
---
- oppositive of MSLT ([[use MSLT to diagnose narcolepsy and hypersomnia]])
- see how long person can resist sleep
- seated upright to stay awake as long as possible
- lasts 40 min and repeats 4x at 2 hour interval
- average: 30 min
