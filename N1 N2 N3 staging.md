# N1 N2 N3 staging

- related: [[Chest lymph nodes and where to find them]]
- tags: #permanent
---

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230708072101.png)

- N1 is ipsilateral hilar lymph nodes
- N2 is involvement of ==ipsilateral mediastinal== lymph nodes
- N3 is contralateral mediastinal or hilar nodes

## Examples

- Left lung nodule
	- 10L or 11L positive: N1
	- 4L positive: N2
	- 7 positive: N2
	- 4R positive: N3
	- 10R or 11R positive: N3
