# tumor lysis syndrome

- related: [[Oncology]]
- tags: #hemeonc
---

## Risks

- [[tumor lysis syndrome risk is highest in leukemia and lymphomas]]

## Pathophysiology

- tumor lysis syndrome occurs as result of large tumor burden undergoing death

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20260117160222.png)

## Labs

- [[tumor lysis syndrome have electrolyte abnormalities]]

## Treatment

- IV hydration is primary treatment
- [[use allopurinol for prophylaxis and rasburicase for treatment of tumor lysis syndrome]]
- initiate dialysis in severe tumor lysis syndrome
