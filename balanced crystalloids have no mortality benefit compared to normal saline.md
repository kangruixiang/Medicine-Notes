# balanced crystalloids have no mortality benefit compared to normal saline

- related: [[fluid management in the ICU]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- Meta analysis in 2019 found no difference between LR and NS. [^1]
- SMART: A pragmatic study involving 15,000 patients at Vanderbilt found for sepsis, LR has much lower mortality (0.74, 95% CI 0.59-0.93), AKI, ventilator free days, RRT free days compared to NS. [^2] [^3]
- Most recent ==Basics trial== found no difference between LR and NS. [^4]
