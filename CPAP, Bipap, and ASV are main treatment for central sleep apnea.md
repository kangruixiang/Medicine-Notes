# CPAP, Bipap, and ASV are main treatment for central sleep apnea

- related: [[Sleep and Sleep Disordered Breathing]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- CPAP is first line treatment for CSA
- CPAP can improve PCO2 slightly by helping VQ mismatch and reducing LV afterload
	- [[CPAP improves LV afterload by preventing upper airway collapse]]
- CPAP does not improve survival benefit in heart failure patients.
	- [[CANPAP trial CPAP does not improve mortality for patients with central sleep apnea and HFrEF?]]

### Bipap

- it's unclear whether bipap is effective
- up to 20% of OSA pts starting on CPAP can also be found to have underlying central events in the first 3 months

### ASV

- adjusts each breath pressure levels
- can increase PS during apnea and hyponea; can also decrease PS during hyperventilation[^1]

[^1]: [[Murray and Nadel Ch 122 Sleep Disordered Breathing Treatment]]
