# dysanapsis in spirometry can be normal or precursor to COPD

- related: [[PFT and lung functions]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---
- dysanapsis is when there is normal FEV1 but decreased FEV1/FVC ratio
- this could be normal physiologic variant (e.g. tall young male with large FVC)
- could also be precursor to actual COPD[^1]

[^1]: [[ERS ATS technical standard on interpretive strategies for routine lung function tests]]
