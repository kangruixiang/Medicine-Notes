# complex fluid on US does not equal to loculation on CT or complicated effusion

- related: [[pleural effusion]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- Seeing loculated fluid on ultrasound is not equivalent to loculation seen on CT lung. Resolution on ultrasound is a lot higher and can see smaller fibrins. Loculated fluid on ultrasound does not mean the fluid cannot be drained with thoracentesis.
- complicated effusion refers to pleural labs labs and not ultrasound finding
	- [[simple effusion, uncomplicated, complicated parapneumonic effusion, empyema]]
