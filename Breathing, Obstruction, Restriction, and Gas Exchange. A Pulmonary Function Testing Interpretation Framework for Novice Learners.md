# Breathing, Obstruction, Restriction, and Gas Exchange. A Pulmonary Function Testing Interpretation Framework for Novice Learners

- related: [[PFT and lung functions]]
- tags: #literature #pulmonology
---

- BORG modules
	- breathing
	- obstruction
	- restriction
	- gas exchange
- breathing
	- lung volumes and capacities
- obstruction
	- definition of obstructiong by FEV/FVC ratio
	- RV > 120 = air trapping
	- TLC > 120 = hyperinflation

- restriction
	- TLC < 80% pred = restriction
	- different lung disease based on pattern of restriction
	- DPLD: [[Diffuse Parenchymal Lung Disease]]
- gas exchange
	- fibrosis: increased alveolar-capillary membrane (ACM) causing decreased exchange
	- COPD: decreased surface area of ACM causing decreased DLCO
	- asthma: increased venous return, capillary blood volume = increased surface area
- Q&A
	- NMD: early = inspiratory muscles affected = lower TLC. Late = expiratory muscle affected
	- obesity: ERV much much lower because of increased abdomen content
	- asthma: decrease in pleural pressure = increased blood pull = more exchange
