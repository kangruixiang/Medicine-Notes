# NSTEMI criteria

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]]
- tags: #literature #cardiology
---

<!-- NSTEMI criteria b:89494284527-->

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250722081054.png)

- ST depression **0.5 mm or more in ≥ 2 contiguous leads**
- horizontal and downslope are more likely
- upslope is less specific
- The larger the depression the more likely.
- OR **T-wave inversion** with pain/discomfort [^1]

[^1]: https://litfl.com/myocardial-ischaemia-ecg-library/
