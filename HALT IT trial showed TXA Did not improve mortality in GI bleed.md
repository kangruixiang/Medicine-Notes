# HALT IT trial showed TXA Did not improve mortality in GI bleed

- related: [[GI bleed]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- [[TXA tranexamic acid has uses for trauma bleed]]
- [[inhaled TXA may be helpful in nonmassive hemoptysis]]
- for patients with acute GI bleed, meta analysis of numerous small trials have not shown improvement in mortality from bleeding. However, TXA group had higher rate of PE and DVT. [^1]

[^1]: HALT-IT Trial Collaborators. Effects of a high-dose 24-h infusion of tranexamic acid on death and thromboembolic events in patients with acute gastrointestinal bleeding (HALT-IT): an international randomised, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial. _Lancet_. 2020;395(10241):1927-1936. doi:10.1016/S0140-6736(20)30848-5
