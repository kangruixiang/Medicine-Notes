# use prothrombin time to rule out dabigatran related bleeding

- related: [[anticoagulation medications and reversal]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- thrombin time has high sensitivity and low specificity for dabigatran
	- [[high sensitivity rules out, high specificity rules in]]
- normal thrombin time is highly sensitive to rule out therapeutic dabigatran therapy
- medications other than dabigatran can cause high thrombin time[^1]

[^1]: Pollack CV Jr, Reilly PA, van Ryn J, et al. Idarucizumab for dabigatran reversal - full cohort analysis. _N Engl J Med_. 2017;377(5):431-441.
