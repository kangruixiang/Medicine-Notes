# amiodarone dosing for atrial fibrillation

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]]
- tags: #permanent

---

## Loading

- start with IV bolus 150. May need 2-3 boluses
- Then 1 mg for 6 hours, followed by 0.5 mg per min for 18 hours
- load with 10g of amiodarone
- Can stop infusion after 24 hours
- amiodarone PO: 7-8 grams
- 400 mg BID until 10 g dosing, and then 200 mg daily
- 400 BID x 7-10 days = 10 gram
- then 200 daily until follow up outpatient
