# plasmaphresis is unique treatment to anti GBM

- related: [[goodpasture syndrome anti GBM]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- prednisone and cyclophosphamide are used to prevent new antibodies
- plasmapheresis is preferred treatment to remove antibodies in goodpastures syndrome, [^1] different from other vasculitis ([[plasmaphresis have no added benefit for ANCA associated vasculitis]])
- rituximab is not more efficacious
