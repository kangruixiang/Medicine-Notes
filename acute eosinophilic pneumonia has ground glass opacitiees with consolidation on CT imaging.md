# acute eosinophilic pneumonia has ground glass opacitiees with consolidation on CT imaging

- related: [[acute and chronic eosinophilic pneumonia AEP and CEP]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- CXR showing more diffuse alveolar opacities:

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230710075428.png)

- CT showing several features
	- ground glass opacities and interlobular thickening being most common
	- most can have pleural effusions
	- consolidations and air bronchogram can also be present[^1]
![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230710075403.png)
