# pulmonary langerhans PLCH etiology, patient population

- related: [[pulmonary langerhans histiocytosis PLCH]]

<!-- PLCH pathogenesis, patient population b:527233063301-->

Pulmonary Langerhans cell histiocytosis (previously histiocytosis X or eosinophilic granuloma) is a rare form of interstitial lung disease that primarily affects ==young adults==.

90% of affected are ==cigarette smokers==.[^2]

PLCH is a rare ==sporadic cystic lung disease== of ==unknown etiology==, seen in young adult smokers, that is characterized by the infiltration and destruction of the wall of distal bronchioles by CD1a-positive Langerhans-like cells.[^1]
