# use idarucizumab Praxbind for dabigatran Pradaxa reversal

- related: [[Hematology]]
- tags: #literature #icu #hemeonc
---

<!-- dabigatran reversal agent b:855767430388-->

- Praxbind (idarucizumab) is a monoclonal antibody Fab fragment that binds dabigatran
- works in 15 min, lasts 24 h
- has no effect on factor 10a inhibitors (heparin) [^1]

---

==Idarucizumab==, a monoclonal antibody fragment that binds ==dabigatran==, neutralizes dabigatran activity, and rapidly reverses the anticoagulant effects. It is the only specific reversal agent currently available for patients taking dabigatran who present with major bleeds. Importantly, it does not have any effect in reversing direct factor Xa inhibitors, including apixaban (choice A is incorrect).
