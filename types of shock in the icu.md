However, it may be easier to remember from head to toe:

- neurogenic
	- trauma
	- spinal anesthesia
- cardiac
	- LV or RV failure
	- MI
	- tamponade
	- LVOT obstruction
	- PE
	- tachycardia or bradycardia
	- Post arrest or MI SIRS
	- anaphylaxis
	- hypovolemia
- pulmonary
	- pneumothorax, high autoPEEP, decompensated pulmonary HTN
- hematologic
	- bleeding
- endocrine
	- adrenal crisis
	- thyroid storm
- GI
	- pancreatitis
	- [[acute abdominal compartment syndrome]]
- infectious
	- septic shock
