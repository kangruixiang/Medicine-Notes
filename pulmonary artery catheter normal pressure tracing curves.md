- RAP: CVP tracing
- RVP: rapid increase in systole with ==slow rise in diastole== (differentiate from PA tracing)
- pulmonary artery: diastolic pressure step up.
	- dicrotic notch appears related to PV pressure
- wedge: similar to CVP with a wave and c wave
