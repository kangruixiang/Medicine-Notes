# CRRT solutions

- related: [[Nephrology]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- Na: usually physiologic
- K: usually 4K bag for all pts except severe hypkerlamia > 6
- 0-2 can be used for severe hyperkalemia
- bicarb: used over lactate
- phos: usually no phos or 1 mmol/L
- glucose: either glucose free or 100
- ca: usually no ca or 2.5-3.5 meq/L ca[^1]

[^1]: https://www.uptodate.com/contents/prescription-of-continuous-kidney-replacement-therapy-in-acute-kidney-injury-in-adults
