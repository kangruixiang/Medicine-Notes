# left axillary arterial line for invasive monitoring

- related: [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #permanent #icu
---
<!-- axillary arterial line location and ultrasound b:1498157399130-->

There's theoretical concern of air bubble causing cerebral embolism when placing right axillary arterial line. Left axillary line may be the better target.[^1]

The axillary artery runs inferior and posterior to ==pectoralis minor== and superior and anterior to ==teres major and lat muscles==.
