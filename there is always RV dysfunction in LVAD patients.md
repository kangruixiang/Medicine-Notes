# there is always RV dysfunction in LVAD patients

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]]
- tags: #permanent
---

LVAD is continuous movement of fluid from LV to aorta. This is stressful for RV. Normal RV will have mild dysfunction, whereas any RV dysfunction will worsen because of LVAD.
