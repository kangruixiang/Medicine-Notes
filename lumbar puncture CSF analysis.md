# lumbar puncture CSF analysis

- related: [[Neurology]]
- literature
---
- [[CSF analysis with over 5 WBC is infectious]]
- [[CSF analysis with lactate over 4 is sign of infection]]
- [[eosinophils in CSF is sign of parasitic infection]]
- [[bacterial meningitis has decreased glucose, normal protein, neutrophilia in CSF]]
- [[viral and fungal meningitis has increased glucose, decreased protein, and lymphophilia in CSF]]
- meningitis abx coverage
- when to add ampicillin
- avoid chlorhexidine for lumbar puncture to avoid aseptic meningitis

# Rule of 20

- bacterial meningitis:
	- opening pressure > 20
	- protein > 200
	- cell count > 2000
	- glucose < 20
