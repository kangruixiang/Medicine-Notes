# Mediastinal Staging for Lung Cancer

- related: [[lung mass and cancer]]
- tags: #literature
---
- PET and CT have 10% false positive and almost 40% false negative.
- Indication for mediastinal staging differ by society but generally have following:
	- tumor > 3 cm
	- lymph node > 1 cm
	- central tumors
	- lymph nodes with positive PET
