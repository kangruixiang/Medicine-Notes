# DEXA ARDS showed improvement in mortality with high dose dexamethasone

- related: [[ARDS acute respiratory distress syndrome]]
- tags: #permanent
---
- pts with ards intubated and ventilated
- 20 mg IV daily for day 1-5 followed by 10 mg IV day 6-10
- significantly decreased mortality and ventilator free days
