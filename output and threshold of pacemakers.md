# Output and Threshold of Pacemakers

- related: [[cardiac pacemaker]]
- tags: #literature #cardiology
---

<!-- what are output and threshold in cardiac pacemakers? How can you tell if output needs to be changed? b:648128034307-->

- output is the amount of mA the machine sends to the wire
- threshold is the minimum mA required to generate depolarization
	- in atrium: p wave
	- in ventricle: QRS
- in cases where there is sensing but not capturing, output needs to be increased:

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250719080847.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250719081118.png)

- permanent pacemaker and temporary pacemakers differ
- permanent pacemaker can adjust both time delivers (volts) and amplitude (mAmps)
- temporary pacemaker can only adjust amplitude
