# ECMO does not show decrease in 60 day mortality for ARDS patients

- related: [[ECMO extracorporeal membrane oxygenation]]
- tags: #permanent
---

EOLIA trial showed that in patients with very severe ARDS, VV ECMO does not improve mortality. [^1]

[^1]: https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa1800385
