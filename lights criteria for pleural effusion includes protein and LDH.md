# lights criteria for pleural effusion includes protein and LDH

- related: [[pleural effusion]]
- tags: #permanent

---

- Lights Criteria
	- Pleural protein >50% serum protein
	- Pleural LDH > 60% of serum LDH (from lysis of neutrophils)
	- Pleural LDH > 2/3 of upper limit of normal serum LDH
- [[diuretic use will cause exudative effusion in heart failure patients]]
- If no serum lab is available, one can also assume normal protein and LDH
	- Pleural protein > 2.9
	- Pleural LDH > 60% upper limit of normal
	- Pleural cholesterol > 45 mg/dL
- Use SAAG for pleural effusion
