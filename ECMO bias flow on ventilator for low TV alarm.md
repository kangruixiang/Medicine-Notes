# ECMO bias flow on ventilator for low TV alarm

- related: [[ECMO extracorporeal membrane oxygenation]]
- tags: #literature #icu
---

<!-- what is bias flow on a ventilator and when is it used? b:563084794362-->

- ECMO patients are usually on very low ventilator settings to rest the lung
- When asthma/COPD patient is on ECMO, there might be very low tidal volume with low ventilator settings due to airway bronchospasm.
- Ventilator may have continuous low tidal volume alarm as result.
- Bias flow (external oxygen bleed in) are often used to avoid low TV ventilator alarm
