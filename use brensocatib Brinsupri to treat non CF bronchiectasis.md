# use brensocatib Brinsupri to treat non CF bronchiectasis

- related: [[chronic bronchiectasis]]
- tags: #literature #pulmonology
---

## Trial

- [[Aspen trial shows brensocatib can be used to improve non CF bronchiectasis]]

## Dosing

- 10 mg, take 1 tablet once daily
- 25 mg, take 1 tablet once daily
- take anytime with or without food. Do not double dose if missed dose

## Side Effects

- gingival and peridontal reactions
- Elevated ALT, AST, ALP

## Prescription

- use inLighten for prescription
- diagnosis code: J47.0, J47.1, J47.9
