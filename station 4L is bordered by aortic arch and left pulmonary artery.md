## EBUS

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230708074828.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230708075905.png)

- on EBUS, navigate to right above carina or immediately in the left main bronchus and look at 10-12 o'clock
- as you come up, look for intersection between pulmonary artery and aorta
- PA will be to the left on screen (inferior) and aorta on the right
