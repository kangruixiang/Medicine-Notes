## dexamethasone

- Recovery trial[^1]
	- 10 days 6mg once daily showed lower mortality rate
	- benefit seen in pts on O2 or ventilated. No benefit on RA pts
- resulted supported by CoDex trial
- Covid STEROID 2 trial found no mortality difference between 6 mg vs 12 mg but maybe higher probability of benefit with 12 mg in high levels of respiratory care patients[^2]
- Hydrocortisone and methylprednisolone have been studied but no real evidence to support
- [[dexamethasone and methylprednisolone do not have mineralocorticoid effects to be used for stress dose steroids]]
