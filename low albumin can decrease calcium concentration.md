# low albumin can decrease calcium concentration

- related: [[electrolyte disorders]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- albumin can change calcium levels
- Ca fall by 0.8 for every 1 g/dL albumin fall

$$
Corrected\space Ca = 0.8 \times (4 - Pt's\space Albumin) + Serum\space Ca
$$

_Note:_ formula assumes albumin units in **g/dL**.[^1]

[^1]: https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/31/calcium-correction-hypoalbuminemia#evidence
