# use AVAPS over bipap for better minute ventilation

- related: [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #permanent
---
- AVAPS is average volume assured pressure support
- AVAPS works like PRVC for noninvasive. It ensures a rate and tidal volume and adjusts pressure
- Initial recommended settign:
	- Vt: 8-10 cc/kg
	- RR: 20
	- EPAP: set for oxygenation
	- IPAP: EPAP + 4 to 25 cmH2O. [^1]

[^1]: https://emcrit.org/emcrit/emcrit-341-avaps-average-volume-assured-pressure-support-nippv-with-alex-bracey/
