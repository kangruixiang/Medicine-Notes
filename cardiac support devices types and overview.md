# cardiac support devices types and overview

- related: [[cardiac supportive devices]]
- tags: #literature #icu #cardiology
---

<!-- what are the overall different types of cardiac support devices? b:1422896071777-->

In general, cardiac support devices are categorized into short term vs long term, left vs right heart, with or without oxygenation support, and ECMO.

- short term LV devices, AKA percutaneous LV assist device
	- [[balloon pump cardiac support]]
	- [[impella cardiac device]]
	- [[tandem heart is transseptal LV ECMO]]
	- LV ECMO
- short term RV devices
	- [[impella cardiac device]]: aka impella RP or impella RP flex
	- [[tandem protek duo is RV Ecmo]]
	- RV ECMO
- long term device, aka Durable LV/RV assist device
	- LVAD, RVAD
	- [[heartmate cardiac device]]
