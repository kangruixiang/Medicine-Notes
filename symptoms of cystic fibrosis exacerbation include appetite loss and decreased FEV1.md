# symptoms of cystic fibrosis exacerbation include appetite loss and decreased FEV1

- related: [[CF cystic fibrosis]]
- tags: #permanent
- source:
---

- cystic fibrosis exacerbation symptoms include weight loss, loss of appetite, decrease in FEV1 by 10% on home spirometry (best out of 3)
- fever is not common
- hemoptysis can be seen with streaks of blood
