# using arterial waveform to estimate stroke volume

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]], [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #literature #cardiology #icu
---
<!-- how is stroke volume estimated with arterial waveform? b:223279714185-->

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250724074130.png)

- assumptions for this to be used
	- flow of blood into artery must equal outflow of blood
	- contractility of vessel relates to amount of blood flows through the vessel
- systole: inflow of blood expands artery and is greater than outflow
- diastole: vessel contracts. Outflow of blood is greater
- Area under curve: represents systole and diastole. Sum can be used to estimate stroke volume[^1]
