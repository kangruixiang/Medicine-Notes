# Bipap is alternative treatment to CPAP if hypoventilation and hypoxemia still persists

- related: [[Sleep and Sleep Disordered Breathing]]
- tags: #permanent
---

- use Bipap if not tolerant to CPAP
- EPAP: adjust for apneas
- IPAP: adjust for hypopnea and reras ([[severity of apnea is dependent on AHI, RDI, and RERA]])
