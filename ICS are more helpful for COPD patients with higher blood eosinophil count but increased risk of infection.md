# ICS are more helpful for COPD patients with higher blood eosinophil count but increased risk of infection

- related: [[COPD chronic obstructive pulmonary disease]]
- tags: #permanent
---

ICS containing inhaler does not seem to have effect for patients with blood eosinophils < 100 cells/uL. Furthermore, patients with lower eosinophils have increased risk of bacterial pneumonia due to presence of proteobacteria.

Patients with high blood eosinophil count (e.g. > 300 cells/uL) seem to have the biggest benefit from ICS.[^1]

[^1]: https://goldcopd.org/2023-gold-report-2/
