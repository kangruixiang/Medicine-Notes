# determining between PA and AP chest xray

- related: [[chest imaging]]
- tags: #pulmonology #literature
---

<!-- how are PA CXRs captured b:1587198811256-->

- patient faces the receptor
- shoulders are rotated anteriorly. Scapula move laterally off lung fields.
