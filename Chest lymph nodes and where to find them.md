# Chest lymph nodes and where to find them

- related: [[pulmonary anatomy]]
- tags: #literature
---

- [[chest lymph nodes are divided into zones and stations]]
- [[use first rib as dividing point between station 1 and 2]]
- [[N1 N2 N3 staging]]
- [[station 4R is bordered by SVC, azygos, and trachea]]
- [[station 4L is bordered by aortic arch and left pulmonary artery]]
- [[azygos vein and pulmonary artery distinguish hilar and mediastinal lymph nodes]]
- [[station 7 is bordered by carina and secondary bronchus]]
- [[station 10R is bordered by azygos and right upper lobe]]
- [[station 10L is bordered by pulmonary artery and left upper lobe]]
- [[station 11L is right after left upper lobe takeoff]]
- [[station 11R has superior and inferior nodes]][^1]

[^1]: [link to IASLC](https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/10.1148/rg.346130097)
