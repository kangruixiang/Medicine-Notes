# IBCC role of procalcitonin in sepsis

- related: [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #literature
- source: [IBCC](https://emcrit.org/ibcc/sepsis/#procalcitonin)

---

- procalcitonin is elevated in bacterial infection and not in viral infection
- more elevated in gram negative infections
- not elevated in atypical infections (mycoplasma)
- procal is good for discontinuing antibiotics and not initiating them
	- high false negatives from neutropenia, localized infection, atypical, late onset
