# hypereosinophilic obliterative bronchiolitis

- related: [[Pulmonary Diseases]]
- tags: #literature
---

- characterstics signs of bronchiolitis on HRCT and biopsy
- AEC > 1000
- BAL eosinophilia > 25% ([[eosinophilic BAL points to atypical infections and atopic conditions]])
- airflow obstruction not improved on inhaled bronchodilators or steroids[^1]

[^1]: [[Association of Pulmonary and Critical Care Medicine Program Directors]]
