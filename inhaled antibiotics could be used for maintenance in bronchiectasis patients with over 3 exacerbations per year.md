# inhaled antibiotics could be used for maintenance in bronchiectasis patients with over 3 exacerbations per year

- related: [[chronic bronchiectasis]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Inhaled antibiotics include:

- aminoglycosides (tobramycin, gentamicin, amikacin)
- fluoroquinolones (cipro, levo)
- aztreonam
- colistin. [^1]

Typically use tobramycin 28 days on and 28 days off.
