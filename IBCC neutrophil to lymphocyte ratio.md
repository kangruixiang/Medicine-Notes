# IBCC neutrophil to lymphocyte ratio

- related: [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #literature
- source: [IBCC](https://emcrit.org/ibcc/sepsis/#neutrophil/lymphocyte_ratio_\(NLR\))

---

- NLR is neutrophil to lymphocyte ratio, either absolute count or percentages
- physiologic stress increases NLR count. This happens faster than WBC or procal level
- exogenous steroid may increase NLR
- likewise, patients with adrenal insufficiency will have lower NLR
