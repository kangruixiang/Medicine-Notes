# apical chamber views on cardiac ultrasound

- related: [[Cardiology and Hemodynamics]]
- tags: #cardiology #literature
---

<!-- what are the apical chamber views on cardiac US? b:1547254684530-->

- apical views on cardiac ultrasound has 4 chambers, 2 chambers, and 3 chambers
- 2 chamber view sees left side of the heart, by ==rotation A4C view 60 degrees CCW==.
- 3 chamber view is done by continue to rotate probe 30-60° CCW from A2C view
	- allows visualization of both MV and aortic root.
