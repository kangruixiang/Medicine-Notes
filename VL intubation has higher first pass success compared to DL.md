# VL intubation has higher first pass success compared to DL

- related: [[intubation in the ICU]]
- tags: #literature #icu
---
<!-- DEVICE trial result -->

In the large, multicenter randomized ==DEVICE trial==, when compared with direct laryngoscopy, ==video laryngoscopy== resulted in ==higher rates of successful intubation on the first attempt==. However, despite this improved first-pass success, ==rates of complications==, including severe hypoxemia, ==were similar== in patients who underwent intubation with video laryngoscopy compared with direct laryngoscopy (choice A is incorrect).
