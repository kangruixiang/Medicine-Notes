# mulch pneumonitis is hypersensitivity in CGD patients

- related: [[ILD Interstitial Lung Disease or Diffuse Parenchymal Lung Disease]]
- tags: #permanent
---
- up to 30% of patients with chronic granulomatous disease (CGD) can have hypersensitivity pneumonitis
- these patients can have high eosinophil count
- image shows consolidations
- biopsy shows granulomas[^1]
