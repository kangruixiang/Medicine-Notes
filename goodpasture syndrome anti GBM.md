# goodpasture syndrome anti GBM

- related: [[Rheumatology]]
- tags: #literature
---

## Demographics

- mostly white patients in 20s-30s and older women in 60s

## Pathogenesis

- small vessel vasculitis
- autoantibody against basement membrane of glomerulus and alveoli

## Symptoms

- usually present with pulmonary hemorrhage and AKI with hematuria, proteinuria
- renal more common than pulmonary
- RPGN: rapidly progressive glomerulonephritis and renal failure
- [[goodpasture differs from GPA with lack of constitutional symptoms]]

## Diagnosis

- Up to 1/3 can have ANCA (usually MPO) antibodies
- diagnosis is suggested by positive anti-GBM antibody
- renal biopsy shows linear IgG immunofluorescence
- [[DAH bronchoscopy shows hemosiderin laden macophages]]

## Treatment

- steroids, cyclophosphamide
- [[plasmaphresis is unique treatment to anti GBM]]
