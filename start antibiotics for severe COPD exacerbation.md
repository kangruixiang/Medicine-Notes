# start antibiotics for severe COPD exacerbation

- related: [[COPD chronic obstructive pulmonary disease]]
- tags: #permanent
---

GOLD guideline recommends given COPD patients antibiotics in following scenarios:

- if patient has clinical signs of pneumonia
- if patients meets 2/3 criteria of COPD exacerbation (increased sputum, increased purulence, increased dyspnea)
- if patient has hx of COPD and is needing NIV/intubation[^1]

[^1]: https://goldcopd.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/GOLD-2023-ver-1.2-7Jan2023_WMV.pdf
