# removing small or large amount of pleural effusion have the same risk of reexpansion pulmonary edema

- related: [[pleural effusion]]
- tags: #permanent
---

There is risk of expansion pulmonary edema any time you remove fluid with thoracentesis or chest tube. However, that risk is the same whether you remove small amount of fluid or large amount of fluid. Therefore, you should always remove as much fluid as possible.
