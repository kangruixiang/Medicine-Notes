# tumor lysis syndrome risk is highest in leukemia and lymphomas

- related: [[tumor lysis syndrome]]
- tags: #literature #hemeonc
---

<!-- which type of cancers have highest risk for tumor lysis syndrome? b:163616986765-->

- different types of cancers have different risk factors for tumor lysis syndrome
- acute leukemia and lymphomas have the highest risks (more cell burden and death)
- solid tumors and multiple myeloma have lower risks
