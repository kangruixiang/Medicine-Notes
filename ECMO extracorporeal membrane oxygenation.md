## VA ECMO

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250721081923.png)

- retrograde blood flow delivered up femoral artery and illiac system is increasing afterload against which LV has to pump
- adding strain to already strained ventricle
- If ventricle still have contractility, can fight against ECMO circuit
- NS syndrome: blood going to brain is less oxygenated than blood going to distal peripheral

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20250721082919.png)

- ecmo and LA venting
- strategies to unload LV to help with pumping[^1]

## Demographics

- [[ECMO does not show decrease in 60 day mortality for ARDS patients]]
- [[ECMO has no effect on survival benefit in shock patients]]
- [[use VA ECMO for VF VT arrest patients]]
- Use RESP Score to calculate mortality
- initiate VV ecmo when P:F < 150 or P:F < 80, refractory hypoxemia or hypercapnia
	- Use RESP score
	- bronchopleural fistula, ARDS, obstruction
- Use Va ecmo for refractory cardiogenic shock or cardiac arrest
	- PE, overdose
	- use SAVE score

## Physiology

- [[ECMO patients are harder to sedate and harder to wake up because of lipophilic circuit?]]
- [[bivalrudin is better than heparin for VV ECMO?]]
	- no 10a levels for bival as it's 2a direct
- [[use low TV and FiO2 on ventilator for ECMO patients]]
- Sweep is amount of CO2 removal
- Flow is PEEP/FiO2
- cannula size: use vessel mm x 3 = French size
- flow needs: 60-80 cc/kg/min
- [[use mixed venous oxygen to guide blood transfusion in ECMO]]
