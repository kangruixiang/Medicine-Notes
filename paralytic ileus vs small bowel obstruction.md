# paralytic ileus vs small bowel obstruction

- related: [[GI gastroenterology]]
- tags: #permanent
---

On xray, paralytic ileus can have colon dilation.

SBO on xray does not have colon dilation.

## Management

- in general SBO requires NG for decompression
- paralytic ileus does not require NG. However, stopping tube feeds clinically may be needed
- erythromycin is not effective for ileus
- neostigmine can be used for colonic dilation or pseudo-obstruction
- limit opioids
- can try enteral naloxone[^1]

[^1]: https://emcrit.org/ibcc/ileus/
