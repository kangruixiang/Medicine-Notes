# sizes in french and gauge

- related: [[ICU intensive care unit]]
- tags: #literature #icu
---
- a lower gauge is larger
- In contrast, a larger French is larger
- common sizes
	- 18G = 3.8 Fr
	- 16G = 5 Fr
	- 7 Fr = 13G
	- 8 Fr = 12G
	- 9 Fr = 11G
- [conversion calculator](http://www-users.med.cornell.edu/~spon/picu/calc/gafrconv.htm)
