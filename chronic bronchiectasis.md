## Treatment

- [[airway clearance and hypertonic saline is key treatment in bronchiectasis]]
- [[azithromycin could be helpful in bronchiectasis patients with over 3 exacerbations per year]]
	- [[azithromycin improves immunity and reduces inflammation]]
	- [[use azithromycin with caution in patients with nonTB mycobacterium infections]]
- [[inhaled antibiotics could be used for maintenance in bronchiectasis patients with over 3 exacerbations per year]]
- [[use albuterol to help improve irritation for inhaled antibiotics]]
- [[eradication of bacteria colonization in bronchiectasis]]
- [[avoid pulmozyme or dornase alfa in non-cystic fibrosis CF bronchiectasis]]
- [[use of brensocatib reduces exacerbation in bronchiectasis]]
- [[bronchiectasis exacerbation is defined by worsening symptoms for 48 hours needing treatment]]
- [[Krall notes for bronchiectasis]]
- [[use brensocatib Brinsupri to treat non CF bronchiectasis]]
