# use stylet or bougie to have better first pass success rates with intubation

- related: [[intubation in the ICU]]
- tags: #literature #icu
---

<!-- how does stylet or bougie affect intubation success rate? b:430781463690-->

- using ==stylet== results higher first pass success rates when performing intubation compared to ET tube alone in STYLETO trial [^1]
- using ==bougie== also increases first pass success rate compared to stylet in BEAM trial but is compared to stylet in BOUGIE trial.[^2]

[^1]: [[Effect of endotracheal tube plus stylet versus endotracheal tube alone on successful first-attempt tracheal intubation among critically ill patients - STYLETO study]]
[^2]: [[Effect of Use of a Bougie vs Endotracheal Tube With Stylet on Successful Intubation on the First Attempt Among Critically Ill Patients Undergoing Tracheal Intubation - A Randomized Clinical Trial]]
