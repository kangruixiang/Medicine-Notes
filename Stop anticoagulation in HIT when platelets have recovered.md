# Stop anticoagulation in HIT when platelets have recovered

- related: [[heparin induced thrombocytopenia and thrombosis HIT]]
- tags: #permanent
---

Duration of anticoagulation in acute HIT patients without VTE is until platelet recovery, >150. Do not treat for > 30 months unless there is persistant HIT. [^1]

Warkentin, Ashleigh Wex, Reem A. Mustafa, Rebecca L. Morgan, Nancy Santesso; American Society of Hematology 2018 guidelines for management of venous thromboembolism: heparin-induced thrombocytopenia. Blood Adv 2018; 2 (22): 3360–3392. doi: https://doi.org/10.1182/bloodadvances.2018024489
