# sarcoidosis patient population and symptoms

- related: [[pulmonary and extrapulmonary sarcoidosis]]
- tags: #permanent #pulmonology
---

## Patient population

- most commonly seen in young african american females, 20-60 years old
- mostly negative family hx[^1]

## Symptoms

- pulmonary involvement: cough, dyspnea
- cardiac: chest discomfort, may not be related to cardiac
- eyes: anterior uveitis in about 25% of patients and symptoms in 5%
- skin: skin lesions, [[erythema nodosum]], [[lupus pernio is sarcoid skin problem and not related to lupus]]
- parotid gland swelling
- constitutional: fatigue, malaise, weight loss, arthralgia, anorexia, fever
- [[Patient with bilateral hilar lymphadenopathy, erythema nodosum, anterior uveitis likely has sarcoidosis]].
- [[genitourinary sarcoidosis and testicular nodules]]
