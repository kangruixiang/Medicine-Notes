# image findings in hypersensitivity pneumonitis include GGO, centrilobular micronodules, and mosaicism

- related: [[HP hypersensitivity pneumonitis]]
- tags: #permanent
- source: [[Fundamentals of High Resolution Lung CT]], [[An Algorithmic Approach to Lung Disease]]
---

## GGO

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230110110408.png)

- ground glass opacity can be predominant findings in subacute HP

## Centrilobular micronodules

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230110110622.png)

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20221204102145.png)

- Hypersensitivity pneumonitis with centrilobular distribution

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20221204102001.png)

- hypersensitivity pneumonitis with centrilobular consolidation, air bronchogram
- air bronchogram differentiates HP from sarcoidosis ([[pulmonary sarcoidosis findings include mediastinal lymphadenopathy and perilymphatic nodules]])

## Distribution

- distribution usually more in mid lung and more central in location as compared to lower lung and peripheral in other interstitial pneumonias

## Chronic

![](https://photos.thisispiggy.com/file/wikiFiles/20230110110758.png)

- fibrosis is finding for chronic HP
- bronchiectasis is more traction than central[^1]
