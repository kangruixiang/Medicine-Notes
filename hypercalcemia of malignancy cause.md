# hypercalcemia of malignancy cause

- related: [[Oncology]]
- tags: #literature #hemeonc
---

<!-- when does hypercalcemia of malignancy happen? b:536723889468-->

- increased PTHrP
	- mostly from sqCC of lung. More localized tumors
- increased osteolysis from bone metastases
	- breast and myeloma
- increased peripheral calcium conversion to 1,25-dihydroxyvitamin D
	- lymphomas such as Hodgkins
- ectopic PTH secretion
	- ovarian cancers[^1][^2]
