# EVD complication includes infection

- related: [[Neurology]]
- tags: #literature #icu
---

<!-- EVD complications -->

The ==most feared complication== of prolonged cerebrospinal fluid drainage via an EVD is ==infection==. Infection is seen in 3% to 70% of cases, depending on the criteria used to determine infection. Unfortunately, ==early removal of the EVD is not commonly an option== due to the subsequent development of hydrocephalus, which can be life-threatening.[^1]
