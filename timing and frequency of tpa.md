# timing and frequency of tpa

- related: [[pleural effusion]]
- tags: #permanent
- source:
---

- [Mist2](https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/nejmoa1012740):
	- tpa followed by 1 hour clamping, followed by DNase, followed by 1 hour clamping
	- BID for 3 days
- [Once daily use of tpa/dornase](https://www.karger.com/Article/FullText/443334)
	- tpa followed by 1 hour clamp, followed by suction, followed by DNase
	- once daily for 3 days
- [Concurrent intrapleural](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/27333122/)
	- tpa and DNase together with 2 hour clamping, followed by suction
	- BID for maximum 6 doses
- [Concurrent vs sequential](https://journals.lww.com/bronchology/Fulltext/2018/04000/Concurrent_Versus_Sequential_Intrapleural.9.aspx)
	- tpa/dnase together, clamp 1 hour, suction 1 hour vs tpa/dnase separate
	- BID for maximum 6 doses
